Making sure women are represented everywhere in TECH...


Women in Technology is a community for females that are aspiring and already have a career in the field. It is common to find very few women in technology and very few on boards of companies. It has also been proven that women earn lower than their male counterparts in various fields, this has been a major contributing factor of poverty in the world. WIT goal is ‘Making sure women are represented everywhere in TECH’. The community seeks to provide support, access to opportunities, mentorships, training/education and the much needed information they need to thrive in the space.
