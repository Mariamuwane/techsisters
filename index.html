Our Projects

Women for Design

This project was carried out in 2021. 500 Women all over Africa were invited and empowered in the art of design in technology. They were taught tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Canva.

Women and Software

This program was done to introduce women without a background in technology, to software development. Over 1000 women across the world were in attendance.

Software and Softskills

Hard and soft skills go hand in hand, one may not succeed without the other. This was explored at the event and women got to know their how both can be applied together.

Women Trailblazers in Tech

Adeola Haruna

A co-founder for Girl code Academy, she has empowered over 100 young women in software development and design.

Odun Ewenyi

The co-founder for Piggyvest and Firstcheck. Odun has pushed for women inclusivity in tech and startups. She has helped to fund women.

Ada Nduka Oyom

The founder of She Code Africa. She has helped to provide young women with access to opportunities and resources.

Eloho Omame

Eloho is a co-founder for Firstcheck. She believes in women startups and invests in them, she also educates about Venture Capital.
